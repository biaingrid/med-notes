|      Aspecto       |                                                     Lei 8.080/90                                                      |                                                             Lei 8.142/90                                                             |
| :----------------: | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: |
